About TOHAI

Who We Are

Tochi Health Access Initiative (TOHAI) is a non-profit organisation dedicated to improving access to genotype education, early screening, referral pathways, and policy advocacy for sickle cell disease prevention and care in Nigeria.

Our Mission

We believe every Nigerian deserves to know their genotype and have access to quality healthcare information. Through community outreach, partnerships with healthcare providers, and policy advocacy, we're working to eliminate preventable cases of sickle cell disease.

Our approach combines education, early screening, comprehensive support systems, and advocacy to create lasting change in communities across Nigeria.
