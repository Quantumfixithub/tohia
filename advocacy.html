Driving policy change for lasting impact. Because sustainable prevention requires systems, not just services.

Our Approach

Why Policy Matters

Sickle cell disease is not just a medical challenge. It is a public policy issue.

Community programmes reach thousands. Policy reform reaches millions. At TOHAI, we believe that sustainable change requires embedding early screening and prevention into Nigeria's public healthcare system ‚Äî not just delivering temporary projects.

Our Policy Goal

TOHAI's ultimate advocacy goal is the national adoption of universal newborn screening for sickle cell disease in Nigeria.

We envision a future where every child born in Nigeria is screened within the first days of life ‚Äî not as a privilege, but as a standard of care.

Policy reform is how we move from helping thousands to protecting millions.
